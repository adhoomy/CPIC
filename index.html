About Our Masjid

The College Point Islamic Center is for the Muslims in the neighborhood and outside to come and pray, and for those looking to learn about Islam as well.

Our mission is to serve as a center for Islamic education, worship, and community engagement. We strive to provide a welcoming environment for Muslims and non-Muslims alike.

Through our various programs and services, we aim to foster a deeper understanding of Islam and build bridges with the wider community.
